Sub-Teams

Making a cutting-edge tiny house is challenging, so we work in six sub-teams, each specializing in a different aspect of the tiny house and the overarching mission.
The Business team is responsible for securing funding, corresponding with the school and other partners, and understanding/sharing tiny house legalese

Business
The Public Relations sub-team coordinates outreach to promote sustainability and maintain a strong social media presence.

Public Relations
The Renewable Energy/Electricity /Smart Home (REESH) teamâ€™s responsibilities include powering the house, setting up internet, and integrating smart home technology.

REESH
The Systems team is responsible for designing and setting up the water and heating/cooling systems. We take care of plumbing, water collection, waste management, insulation, ventilation, and temperature control.

Systems
The Structural Design subteam designs and builds the framework of the house. This includes the framing, sheathing, interior walls, siding, and roofing of the house. Additionally, we make sure the house is structurally sound and choose materials.

Structural Design
The Interior Construction team designs and furnishes the interior of the Tiny House to get the most out of a small space.
